# PRD – HRMS SaaS Platform

## 1. Overview
A multi-tenant HRMS SaaS platform designed to manage the complete employee lifecycle, from recruitment to offboarding, with strong focus on automation, analytics, and mobile-first operations.

## 2. Target Users
- HR Teams
- Managers
- Employees
- Admins (Platform owners)

## 3. Core Objectives
- Replace fragmented HR tools
- Provide real-time workforce visibility
- Enable automation of HR workflows
- Support mobile-first field workforce
