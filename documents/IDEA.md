# IDEA – HRMS SaaS Platform

## 1. Problem

HR systems today are:
- Fragmented (multiple tools)
- Not mobile-friendly
- Poor in real-time tracking
- Weak in automation
- Complex to customize

Field workforce tracking is especially broken:
- No real-time visibility
- No geo-based attendance
- Manual reporting

---

## 2. Opportunity

Build a unified platform that:
- Handles entire employee lifecycle
- Works mobile-first
- Supports real-time tracking
- Automates workflows
- Provides deep analytics

---

## 3. Solution

A modular HRMS SaaS platform with:
- API-first backend (Frappe)
- Modern frontend (Next.js)
- Mobile apps
- Multi-tenant support

---

## 4. Target Market

- SMBs (50–1000 employees)
- Field workforce companies
- Startups scaling HR operations

---

## 5. Scope (MVP)

### Phase 1:
- Employee Management
- Attendance (with geo)
- Leave
- Basic Dashboard

### Phase 2:
- Recruitment
- Request Desk
- Reporting

### Phase 3:
- Performance
- Training
- Surveys

### Phase 4:
- Advanced analytics
- AI automation

---

## 6. Differentiation

- Mobile-first HR
- Geo-tracking native
- Modular architecture
- Developer-friendly backend

---

## 7. Risks

- Overbuilding too early
- ERP-level complexity
- Performance scaling

---

## 8. Strategy

- Build core first
- Launch early MVP
- Iterate based on usage
